About the initiative

Hacqs initiative Ghana, is a non-profit / non-government organisation established on March 1, 2020. This organisation was established to impact in the life of others.

Our Mission

Hacqs initiative Ghana will contribute to our communities mainly through the areas of Basic Education(illiteracy), Information and Communication Technology, and HealthCare

Our Vision

With the help of the Government of Ghana and other Non Governmental Organisations, we will want to have a country with low illiteracy rate and poverty rate.
